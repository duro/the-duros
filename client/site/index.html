Our Story

Two lovers on a collision course. That best describes Torie and Adam's meeting. Never has their been two people more perfectly matched for each other. Each have led challenging, interesting, and vibrant lives before they met, and when these two lives finally came toghether, it was nothing short of meant to be.

Both artists, both romantics, both passionate, and both beautiful and bright spirits. With all their many similarities, this beautiful union could not be as stong as it is without their individuality and passion for what makes them happy. Torie grew up on the Westside of LA, while Adam grew up in the nature of Sedona, AZ. Two seperate paths, ultimately heading for one.

On a Los Angeles night, like any other, Torie sat with friends. From across the room, she sees a man with blue eyes. As he stands to make an announcement, things around her get quiet, an energy puls her attention towards him, and she thinks to herself, "I want to know him."

Day, weeks, months pass. The two lover's paths cross, and with each meeting they discover more and more how drawn togehter they are. Then one day, out of the blue, he asks her for coffee. Something she never thought would happen, something he knew was what he had always wanted to do.

From that day on, they could not deny that the other was the person they had always dreamed of walking through life with. Their love, commitment, and joy inspires their own life, and the lives of those around them

Two years later, on a beautiful Friday in September, the two lovers spend the day together laughing, shopping, and taking in their favorite passtime, spending quality time with each other. After a lovely dinner, they walk hand in hand to the Santa Monica pier, where they had their first date. After a stroll, Adam turns to Torie and asks her to board the Ferris Wheel with him. After some hesitation, heights not being her favorite, Torie agrees. She does not regret pushing past her fear, as at the top of the wheel, he bends to one knee, reaches into his pocket, and those words she had always hoped would come leave his lips... "Will you marry me?"
